Short Bio

I'm a PhD student at Johns Hopkins University. My advisor is Bloomberg Distinguished Professor Alan Yuille. My research interests are computer vision and medical imaging, especially in exploring computer vision algorithms for real world clinical problems. I had chance to intern at PAII (2019), Nvidia (2018) and MSRA (2017). I obtained my B.E. at Tsinghua University in 2017.
